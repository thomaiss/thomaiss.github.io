Road Game Innit

Road Game Innit is the first game that I ever created. It was made using Pygame and it took me around 30 hours or so to create. You play as a mail van collecting parcels and dodging traffic cones. Every parcel you get increases your score by 1. That is the entire game. This game is also the origin story for my traffic cone persona.


You can download the game if you'd like to play it yourself.
